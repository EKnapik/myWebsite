Starting Fluid Simulation

Learning fluid simulations from the ground up. Smooth particle hydrodynamics. One of the most interesting things so far is the Kernal functions and the semi-arbitaryness of these, just that they work. Just some basic notes here is that smooth particle hydrodynamics (SPH) works by examining the fluid around a particular particle then makes adjustments with the timestep to your favorite integration method. People tend to use Semi-implicit Eulerian because it's stable and 2d order accurate. (The simulation is done just havn't finalized the webpage for this yet)
